Share your digital photos with friends and family! With {PLATFORM} you get easy to use, out of the box photo album! All you need is your digital pictures!

Add Album viewers

Click the menu item Album viewers to add users for private viewing of photo albums.

Add albums

Every user on {PLATFORM} can create and publish photo albums, except for the administrator.

  1. Copy the folders containing your pictures to \{PLATFORM}\storage\pictures, with for example the built in File Manager or Windows Explorer.
  2. Log in as your standard user on {PLATFORM}.
  3. Click 'File Manager'.
  4. Browse to /home/storage/pictures.
  5. Select the folders or pictures you wish to add as photo albums.
  6. Select the 'Add to album' icon in the file function bar.
  7. Open the Album manager. Decide if the added albums should be public or private. By default all added albums are private.
  8. Click your album, on the left side of the screen.
    • Ether select the users who you would like to share the album with, then click Update.
    • Or click 'Public' if you want the album to be open to anyone who can access your {PLATFORM}, then click Update.
  9. Done!

Edit albums

Click the album you wish to edit.

  • To edit album- or picture caption select the object, enter text and click 'Update'.
  • To delete an album, select the album and press 'Delete album'.
  • To delete a picture, select the picture and press 'Remove from album'.

Your photo albums will be visible by browsing to http://{DEFAULT_HOST}/album (http://{DEFAULT_HOST}.local/album if using Mac) from inside your home network, or by browsing to www.-your easyfind name-.{EASYFIND} from outside your network.
